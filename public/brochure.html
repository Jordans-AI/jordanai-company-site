Discovery Questions - Our First Meeting Guide

To understand your business and identify the best opportunities for AI integration, we'll explore these key areas together:

1.
Tell me a bit about your business - what does it do, who are your customers, and what is your main workflow?
2.
What are the three areas where your business wastes the most time?
3.
What tasks repeat themselves in your business? (Reports, customer management, marketing, operations, communication, service, CRM...)
4.
What feels stuck or slow in your business today?
5.
If you could "press a button" and improve something - what would it be?
6.
How do you work with information today? (Files, spreadsheets, CRM, project management...)
7.
Do you have experience with AI tools already? What worked and what didn't?
8.
What's most important for you to achieve in this process - time savings? cost savings? improved service? increased sales?
9.
If we decide to move forward and work together - who do you think is the right person or team we should work with, and who we can train to ensure smooth and efficient implementation?
10.
Are there systems or infrastructure you're committed to? (Regulations, data security, servers, cloud, existing tools that can't be changed)
